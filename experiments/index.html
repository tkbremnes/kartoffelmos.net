Experiments

This is where I'll put everything dumb, cool but useless doodads, things that are too buggy to be used for anything, and unfinished stuff I'm not working on actively any more. Most experiments are optimised for a single browser (build). URLs are in constant flux (which I may or may not do something about).

  • Gardin – For the occations where the marquee-tag was did not satisfy you. Optimised for Chrome
  • Bubble – Interactive plot diagram implemented with a canvas element.
  • Zine – Zelda fanzine, containing nothing but gibberish and some images. Optimised for the Opera Reader build.
  • Boo – Cursor hover effects experiment. Still undecided if subtle portrait movements, Harry Potter style, is creepy or cool. Optimized for Opera
  • Cellulose – My very first experiment with pagination. With video, pictures, banners, and interactive canvas elements to create a multimedia experience. Optimised for the Opera Reader build.
  • Moira – Something slightly blog-esque. More a late night design experiment than anything else. Astonishingly unfinished. Works (experimentally) in the Opera Reader build, as well as everywhere modern (I think).
  • Spinning icons – Testing out several hover effects. One of these got promoted for use on the main splash page. Optimised for Chrome.
  • Neon – For the times where you just want to cuddle up in the sofa under a blanket and watch a burning neon light. Experimenting with manipulating the audio tag through JavaScript. Optimised for Chrome.
  • Organic – Slightly more mature blog design. Exists in purgatory due to lack of available time. Strong language due of use of Samuel L. Ipsum.
